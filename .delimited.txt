Format of delimited Rubric:
===========================

title	content
plaintext	content
criteria	textbox	label
criteria	radio	label	option1label	option1points	option2label	option2points


Example:
============================

title	Project #1 Grade
plaintext	This is your first project. It's worth 20 points.
criteria	radio	Print "hello world"	Excellent	20	Poor	0
plaintext	Next week you need to work with a partner, who will be assigned to you
criteria	textbox	Your partner for your next assignment
